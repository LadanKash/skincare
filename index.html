vegan

Vegan skincare is free from animal products, animal by-products, and pesticides that animals encounter at farms. Animal by-products such as carmine, gelatin, beeswax, collagen, cholesterol, squalane, and lanolin are popular in many traditional skincare products.

cructry-free

Vegan products are also never tested on animals, meaning that they are considered cruelty-free. This means that no animal is harmed during the production process of a vegan lotion, cleanser, or facial mask.

recyclable

It makes you a more responsible customer because vegan makeup often comes in recyclable, biodegradable, and non-plastic packaging. By picking a vegan brand, you're making a conscious effort to become a more ethical consumer as well as to live a more sustainable lifestyle.
